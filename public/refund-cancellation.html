Refunds and cancellation

Last updated: 18 July 2026

OrderSOUNDS Desk subscriptions are sold and processed by Paddle, our merchant of record. This policy explains how renewals, cancellations, and refund requests work for Desk subscriptions.

Recurring subscriptions

Desk is available on monthly and yearly recurring plans. Unless canceled, a subscription renews automatically at the end of each billing period using the payment method held by Paddle.

Canceling a subscription

You may cancel at any time from the billing portal available in your Desk settings or from the manage-subscription link in your Paddle receipt. A standard cancellation takes effect at the end of the current paid billing period. You keep access until that date and no further renewal is charged.

Refund requests

Refund eligibility depends on applicable consumer law, the circumstances of the purchase, and Paddle's buyer terms. To request a refund, use the manage-purchase link in your Paddle receipt or visit Paddle Buyer Support. You may also email ordersoundsapp@gmail.com so we can help identify the purchase and investigate product-access issues.

Technical or access problems

If a paid subscription does not unlock the expected Desk access, contact us promptly. We will investigate the transaction and entitlement records and either restore the purchased access or help route an eligible refund request.

Statutory rights

Nothing in this policy limits any non-waivable cancellation, withdrawal, or refund rights available under the laws that apply to you. Where those rights provide greater protection, they take precedence.
